Transformation Life Church

Web Design | UI | UX | Rebranding | Development

TLC Transformation Life Church contacted me with a goal of rebranding the church website to bring in a more youthful crowd. After presenting a new brand concept, I was asked to chair the entire rebranding project for the church. Using my knowledge of coding and UI/UX principles in conjunction with my previous event planning skillset I worked to redesign everything from the church website, logos & branding, and even the redecoration of the church. The rebrand increased the youth attendance from 10% to 30% in under 90 days.
