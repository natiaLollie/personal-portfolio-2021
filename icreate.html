iCreate

Web Design ... In Progress

iCreate Child Development is an Infant to Scholar development program whose mission is to provide quality childcare while cultivating an environment of growth. The owner of the childcare center reached out to me in hopes of creating a playful yet structured website that showcased the center's programs and events; while also providing a secure place for parents to access a forum, newsletter, and make payments. I conducted intensive research studying the client's mission, online/offline presence, and customer base to create a unique website design that fit the business needs and the clients wants. iCreate is a startup childcare center and the website is set to launch in 2020.
