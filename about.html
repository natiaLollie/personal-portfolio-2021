About Me

Howdy there! I am Natia Lollie, a designer and developer.I have a passion for coding and designing content for both web and mobile devices; I got my first start into designing and programming at just 16 years old. My little brother and I would spend all of our after school time making modifications to the video games that we played. As time went on that knowledge began to turn into small business opportunities; as friends and family asked for me to design custom websites and marketing materials for them.

Since that time, I have been eagerly learning all things design, user interface/experience, and programming; in order to build eye-catching, easy to use, responsive content that is tailored to my clients customer base. I apply everything that I discover into my projects and I am passionate about doing my very best with each project that I take on.
